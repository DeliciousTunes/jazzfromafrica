Welcome

Meet us at JazzAhead Bremen

We are a collaborative community of individuals, companies, radio promoters, journalists, festival organizers, artists agencies, and managers united by our love for Africa and its jazz. With heart, soul, and a commitment to excellence, we dedicate ourselves to the promotion of this vibrant genre. Join us in our journey to harmonize the rich tapestry of African jazz at JazzInAfrica.org.
